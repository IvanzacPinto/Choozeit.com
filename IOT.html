IOT & Integration

The move from IPV4 to IPV6 was just the beginning. For those of you who are clueless about what that means, in layman terms, the internet is now capable of having as many IP addresses as there are grains of sand in the Desert! Why do we need so many IP addresses?


The simple answer is 'Devices'. If we can tag every single object, then we can enable communications between those objects. With communication, intelligence can be crafted or analytics be applied and then life will be easier to manage? I leave that for you to ponder..


But I paint a really broad picture which is encumbered by 2 basic problems:

  1. Battery Life
  2. Data Deluge


Untill we get past the 1st point, we can bring down the scale of things and work on 'giving a mouthpiece' to some of our everyday items that will help improve your business intelligence or your daily life.
